What We Offer

Marriage / বিবাহ

Marriage/Biye is a hindu sacred ritual which makes a seamless realtion between husband and wife where bride and groom take oath of togetherness in the presence of Lord Vishnu and all the guardians of both the sides.

Annaprashan / অন্নপ্রাশন

First Rice Ceremony(Annaprashan) is an elaborate ceremony called the MukheBhat (meaning "rice in the mouth") or MamaBhat (or "maternal rice"), where the child's maternal uncle or maternal grandfather feeds them rice for the first time.

Griha Prabesh / গৃহ প্রবেশ

A Gruha Pravesh ceremony, also known as gruhapravesam or the housewarming ritual, is a Hindu puja ceremony conducted when one first enters a new home to purify the atmosphere and safeguard the property from evil energy.

Upanayan / উপনয়ন

Upanayan, is a hindu ritual of initiation of brahman social class that makrks the male child's entrance upon the life of a student(Brahmochari) and his acceptance as a full member of his religious community.

Shanti Swastayan / শান্তি স্বস্তয়ন

Shanti Swastayan Puja in hindu sashtra is a way to perform Puja and seek blessings from Supreme god on any need be it natural calamity, illness, loss of peace or removing any evil power from onself or his/her family.
